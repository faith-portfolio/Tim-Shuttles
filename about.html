About Us

Tim Shuttles is a Proudly South Africa based Shuttle company which was born from our taxi business, In 2009 It became official. We are diverse and currently have interests in Car Rental, Shuttle, Tours, Chauffeur and Accommodation Booking services

We are grateful of the opportunity given to us by HLSP.( division of Mott mc Donald It is through their trust on us that Tim Shuttle was able to grow To date we are proud, we are servicing more than five institutions. We're are looking in the five years to be one of the premier and most reliable service providers Our fleet are well looked after, we have more than 10 vehicles of different make to meet customer demands

Tim Shuttles aims to offer a professional service, which means that all our drivers have the appropriate accreditation, offering you peace of mind. We believe that you are the most important person in our business, so whatever your requirements are, please contact us, and we will gladly customise any package or service to meet your specific requirements.
